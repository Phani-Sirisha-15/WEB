    MY NAME

    PHANI SIRISHA

    The name **Phani Sirisha** carries a beautiful blend of cultural and symbolic meanings. - **Phani** (फणी) is of Sanskrit origin and means *snake* or *serpent*. It is often associated with wisdom, protection, and power. In Hindu mythology, the serpent is a sacred and symbolic creature, representing the cycle of life and death, as well as the Kundalini energy in yogic traditions. - **Sirisha** (సిరిష) is derived from Telugu and means *Sacred Tree* or *flower*. When combined, **Phani Sirisha** becomes a powerful and graceful name that symbolizes a harmonious balance between strength and wisdom (Phani) and beauty and purity (Sirisha).
